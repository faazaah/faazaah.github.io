What is a Memory Game?

Also known as card-matching game

The game starts with all cards flipped face down. The player has to click on a card to reveal it. Two cards can be clicked at a time.The player has to remember the card, and open up another matching card. If the cards match , they remain open, and if not, then the cards flip face down again. The player wins the game when all cards are matched.
